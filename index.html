Create a Trust wallet Account. Then send your BNB from your exchange of choice to your Trust wallet. Trust wallet Download Link Apple Store or Play Store

Step 3: Swap BNB for Smart Chain BNB

This can easily be done when viewing the BNB balance in your Trust wallet.

Step 4: Swap $BNB For $LSC on PancakeSwap
